Level Up Your Singing Skills?

It takes time and lots of effort to learn and perfect a new skill. “Singing is a learned skill.” Each student must be motivated and exhibit determination to improve individual performance capabilities. Therefore, practice is key. Practicing includes both under a teacher’s supervision and in individual private practice. Time set aside for 2 to 3 practice sessions each week will allow the student to build their voice and make progress.
